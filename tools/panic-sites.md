`ProcessGrant::enter` calling `Option::unwrap` on the result of entering
the grant region.  These are covered by Flux's `all_enterable`
precondition on the driver structs and are expected.

inside `earliest_alarm` / `get_expirations`, rooted in the `FilterMap`
blocker documented in `trusteds.md` (Type 3: Flux cannot push
`enter_grant_returns_ok` through `FilterMap`).  Marked
`#[flux_rs::trusted]`.

**`button::fired` → `button.rs:260`** — `panic_bounds_check` on
`self.pins[pin_num as usize]` in the lazy-interrupt-disable path.  Marked
`#[flux_rs::trusted_impl]`; safe because `pin_num` comes from
`InterruptValueWrapper::set_value(i as u32)` during initialization, so
it is always a valid index.

**Conclusion:** zero unexplained panics in the Flux-checked drivers.

documented trusted annotation.
